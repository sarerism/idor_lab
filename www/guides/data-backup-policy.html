üîß Backup Methods

Full Backup

Complete copy of all designated data. Performed weekly on all critical systems during maintenance windows.

Incremental Backup

Copies only data that has changed since the last backup. Performed daily to minimize backup time and storage requirements.

Snapshot Backup

Point-in-time copies of virtual machines and storage volumes. Created before major system changes.

üíø Storage Locations

  • Primary Backup Storage: On-site NAS systems with RAID protection
  • Secondary Storage: Off-site data center replication
  • Archive Storage: Cloud-based long-term retention

üîÑ Recovery Procedures

Recovery Time Objectives (RTO)

  • Critical Systems: 4 hours
  • Standard Systems: 24 hours
  • Non-critical Systems: 48 hours

Recovery Point Objectives (RPO)

  • Mission-critical data: 1 hour
  • Important data: 24 hours
  • Standard data: 48 hours

‚úÖ Testing and Verification

Backup integrity is verified through:

  • Automated backup completion checks
  • Monthly test restores of random data samples
  • Quarterly full disaster recovery drills
  • Annual comprehensive system recovery tests

üë• Roles and Responsibilities

  • IT Manager: Review backup reports and approve policy changes
  • Department Heads: Identify critical data requiring backup
