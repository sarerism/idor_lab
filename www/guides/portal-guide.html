Forgot Password?

Click on "Forgot Password?" on the login page. You'll receive a reset link at your registered email address.

Can't Access Portal?

  • Verify you're using the correct Employee ID format
  • Check if CAPS LOCK is enabled
  • Ensure you're on the company network or VPN
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies

Account Locked?

After 5 failed login attempts, your account will be locked for security. Contact IT Help Desk to unlock.
